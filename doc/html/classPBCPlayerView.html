Detailed Description

A graphical representation of a football player.

This includes the player's "body", motion and route. They are all QGraphicItems which are combined as an QGraphicsItemGroup. Instances of PBCPlayerView are typically displayed in PBCPlayView and PBCCustomRouteDialog.
